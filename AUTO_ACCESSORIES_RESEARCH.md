**Content angle:** "My car smells like the Burj Khalifa lobby" | ASMR unbox + first sniff | Smell test reaction. Dark luxury aesthetic. 15-second sensory hook.

**Channel:** [D2C] primary

1. **Lowest capital to start**: ₹75,000 for 500 units

3. **Best content story**: "Made in [Arabia/Bangalore/India]" — fragrance is a story product

The content does not need to show the car. It needs to show the RITUAL: morning, keys in hand, perfume clipped, cabin fills with scent, drive begins. That Reel sells itself.
