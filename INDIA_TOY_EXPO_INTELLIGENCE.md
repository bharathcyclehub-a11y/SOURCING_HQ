## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY — Three insights

**Insight A — There are exactly two B2B toy expos that matter in India, plus one regional B2C giant.**
**Toy Biz International** (TAI, Pragati Maidan/Bharat Mandapam Delhi, every July; 350+ exhibitors 2024 → 400+ 2025 → ~500 expected 2026) and **Kids India** (Spielwarenmesse, Bombay Exhibition Centre Mumbai, every September/October; 115-127 exhibitors, 170+ brands, 5,000+ visitors from 31 countries). Plus **Hyderabad Kids Fair** (HITEX, twice yearly, 17th and 18th editions in 2025; B2C-leaning, ~76 exhibitors, 20K footfall). IKPE 2024 (Jio World Mumbai, Nov 28-30) was a one-off; **postponed for 2025**. The DPIIT-led "India Toy Fair" was virtual-only in 2021 with 1,000+ exhibitors; **no physical 2022-2026 successor edition has materialised**. Bottom line: if BCH attends two events/year, attend Toy Biz Delhi July + Kids India Mumbai October.

**Insight B — The biggest signal of 2026 is that funded D2C is finally arriving in RC, electronic, and ride-on toys.** Three Indian-headquartered toy companies took meaningful venture rounds in the last six months that BCH must monitor: **Mirana Innovations** (Pune, ₹57.5Cr Series A Nov 2025 — already in BCH directory), **Aditi Toys** (Rajkot, ₹36Cr GVFL-led Mar 2026 — NEW; 280 SKUs incl. die-cast, electric, **ride-on**, STEM; 2.25 lakh sqft factory; OEMs for Hasbro/FirstCry/Amazon/Miniso), and **Legend of Toys** (Bangalore, ₹21Cr pre-Series A — same-city peer; RC drift cars, off-road RC trucks, 1:64 tabletop drift, ₹1,599-₹8,799; ₹30Cr ARR in 18 months). Aditi Toys and Legend of Toys are **both NEW to BCH's master data** and both directly RC/ride-on relevant.

**Insight C — TAI's member directory yielded ~120+ verified manufacturer names this session.** The biggest payoff was not the official Toy Biz exhibitor lists (which are gated/PDF-only and consistently 403/ECONNREFUSED for kidsindia.co.in and indiatoyfair.in), but the **TAI member directory at tai-india.org/members?member_type=manufacture**, which is paginated and publicly browsable. From that source plus trade-press cross-reference, **38 NEW manufacturer names** were added to BCH's master data, ranked by RC/ride-on/electronic relevance.
