## PART 1 — INDIAN FABRIC HUBS

### BHILWARA, RAJASTHAN — India's Suiting Capital

60% of all suiting fabric in India made here. 500+ units, 12,500 looms, ~5 crore meters/month. Raymond and every major Indian brand sources here.

**Key Markets:** New Cloth Market (Azad Market), Swadeshi Market, RIICO Industrial Area (Pur Road), Gandhi Nagar

**Ranjan Fabrics Pvt. Ltd.**
- Address: F-181, RIICO Industrial Area, Pur Road, Bhilwara 311001
- Phone: **+91 94133 55735**
- Website: suitingfabrics.com
- Fabrics: Cotton Suiting, Polyester Suiting, Viscose, Blended
- Price/m: ₹65–500 (TR blends); ₹485 for imported TR
- MOQ: 100m+ | Established: 1985, 15 million meters/year capacity
- USB: Mill-direct from India's suiting heartland. The fabric behind every Indian branded suit.

**Akshat Textile Mills**
- Address: Shop G36, Siddhi Complex, Pur Road, Bhilwara 311001
- Website: akshattextilemills.com
- Fabrics: Polyester Viscose (TR), Satin, Suiting & Shirting
- Price/m: ₹65–200 (standard TR), ₹485 (imported premium TR)
- MOQ: 50–100m

**Insight:** Standard TR averages ₹65–110/m. For the premium play, look for wool-rich or poly-wool blends — same RIICO belt, different vendors. The arbitrage here is enormous — fabric at ₹300/m → bespoke suit at ₹15,000+.

### LUDHIANA, PUNJAB — Woolen Fabric Capital

India's largest wholesale market for woolen cloth. Best for winter suiting, wool blends, blazer-weight wool, tweed.

**Key Markets:** Ghumar Mandi Bazar, Chaura Bazar, Purana Bazar / Ghas Mandi, Karimpura Bazar, Akal Market / Mochpura Bazar

**V.S. Fabrics**
- Location: Wool Market, Mochpura Bazar, Ludhiana
- Fabrics: Woolen Knitted Fabric, Dotted Woolen, Printed Woolen
- Price/m: ₹200–800 depending on wool content
- MOQ: 50–100m
- USB: Woolen knit fabrics for winter blazers and unstructured jackets — rare in retail

## PART 5 — IBRAHIM'S 3 UNIQUE ANGLES

### Angle 1: 4-Way Stretch Bespoke Suit (Wide Open Gap)
No bespoke tailor in Bangalore currently offers stretch-fabric custom suits. Fabric from Surat (₹400/m), tailored in Bangalore, positioned as "travel suit." Target: young tech professionals. ₹8,000–15,000 suit that competes with Zara at half the price. Instagram content: wear the suit, jump, stretch, sit on floor — fabric moves with you.

### Angle 2: Italian Fabric Micro-Luxury
Source VBC or Draper's fabric from Vaibhav Overseas Delhi. Commission a master tailor in Bangalore. Sell as "Italian fabric, Bangalore hands." ₹35,000–60,000 ticket. 3–4x margin. Built for an audience of 1,000 serious buyers, not 1,00,000.

### Angle 3: Dobby Jacquard Shirt Brand
₹215/m fabric from Surat → ₹400 to make → ₹1,499–2,999 D2C brand shirt. Content: textured flat lays, the "feel" is everything. Wear it in a café. Show the weave up close. India has no dominant D2C men's premium shirt brand at this price point.
