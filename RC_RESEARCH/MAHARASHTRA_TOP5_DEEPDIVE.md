## 🧭 CROSS-VENDOR PATTERN

- **Most accessible founder:** Mirana — Devansh Sharma + Ravi Yadav fully on LinkedIn, OEM page is a real B2B door, Series A press creates a warm opening. Bharat Hobby's Himanshu Patel is #2.
- **Hardest to reach:** Hemani Exim — zero LinkedIn, zero IG funnel, Gujarati family business that runs on relationship and walk-in. Phone is the ONLY door. Daddy Drones #2 hardest — the IG persona is shielded.
- **Clearest published wholesale:** Only **Mirana** has a formal published OEM/dealer pathway (miranatoys.com/oem + store-locator). Everyone else is "call us" — informal sub-wholesale at retailer markup. **This is the structural opportunity:** BCH can write its own dealer-terms ask to Bharat Hobby + Daddy Drones because they have no published program to anchor to.
- **Drift sub-₹2,500 gap:** None of these 5 sell drift in the ₹800-₹2,500 bracket except Mirana (Watch Tracer / Watch Drifter at toy-tech prices). The other 4 floor at ₹6K+ hobby-grade. **Confirms BCH's positioning gap: there is no Maharashtra player serving the ₹800-₹2,500 drift bracket — Mirana is the closest, and even they are toy-grade gesture-RC, not drift-tuned.**
