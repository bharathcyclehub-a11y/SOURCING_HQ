### 3.4 Cost mathematics

| Mold type | Material | Lifespan | Cost | Lead time |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Prototype (1-cavity) | Aluminum | 1K-10K shots | **₹1.5-4 L** | 3-4 weeks |
| Production (1-cavity P20 steel) | P20 HRC 28-32 | 100K-500K shots | **₹4-12 L** | 6-8 weeks |
| Production (2-cavity P20) | P20 | 100K-500K shots | **₹7-18 L** | 8-10 weeks |
| Production (4-cavity hot-runner) | H13 hardened | 1-5M shots | **₹20-35 L** | 10-14 weeks |

**Per-piece molding cost (ABS, 150g part):**
- 5,000-unit batch: ₹25-40/piece molding + amortized tool ₹120 = **~₹145-160/piece**
- 25,000-unit batch: drops to **₹39-49/piece**
- 100,000-unit batch (sweet spot): **₹18-26/piece**

Add ~30% for paint + finishing + decals = realistic ₹50-65/body at 5K, ₹25-35 at 100K.

**Total Phase 3 first mold + first batch:** ₹9-16.5 L (realistic mid-point ₹10-12 L).

### 4.4 PLI + customs advantage

- Toy imports: **70% customs + 18% GST** since Feb 2020 → ~₹2,000 duty on a ₹2,800 China BOM
- MII toy product: **GST only at 12% HSN 9503**
- **Net landed-cost advantage: ~60%** for MII vs grey-import China
- Koppal Toy Cluster (Karnataka — same state): 5-10K sq ft units, shared CFC mold-shop + painting + packaging
- PLI for Toys: ₹3,489 Cr outlay; ₹5 Cr capex threshold; BCH qualifies once Phase 3.3 hits

### 4.5 Per-unit margin progression

| Path | Landed cost | Margin @ ₹6,000 MRP |
|---|---|---|
| **(a) Full China import (DDP)** | ₹5,010 | ₹990 = **16.5%** |
| **(b) BCH mixed assembly (50% MII)** | ₹4,800 | ₹1,200 = **20.0%** |
| **(c) Full Made-in-India ~80%** | ₹4,200 | ₹1,800 = **30.0%** |

Each step toward MII = **+7 percentage points** of unit margin AND unlocks PLI claim-back. **At 80% MII the unit margin is 2x the pure-import path.**

## 8 · BRAND POSITIONING — THE THESIS

**"Pocket India RC: Made for the Cars We Worship."**

The Indian RC consumer today buys:
- Sub-₹2K toy-grade plastic (disposable, no story)
- ₹14-16K MJX Hyper Go (great spec, no India brand)
- ₹15-25K Kyosho/ARRMA (true hobby, niche)

There is no premium-positioned **₹5-9K, licensed-Indian-IP, packaging-led, content-marketed, in-stock** option.

**The Pocket play:**
- **Product:** 1:14 to 1:10 scale licensed RC cars of the vehicles Indians worship (Gurkha → Bolero → Thar → Defender → BMW M3 over 4 years)
- **Quality:** Made-in-India 46% → 80% over 5 years, BIS-certified, with hobby-grade electronics
- **Price:** ₹5,990-9,990 MRP with 20-30% margin progression
- **Packaging:** Magnetic-close premium boxes (₹40-80/box delta = ₹2K perceived value lift)
- **Service:** Spare-parts ecosystem (motors, batteries, wheels as SKUs) — none of LoT/Mirana/Tygatec do this
- **Marketing:** Content-first (steal LoT's playbook), Bangalore-North retail anchor, Hindi-English code-switch
- **Channels:** D2C (pocketrccars.com) + BCH retail + Hamleys + Amazon premium
