## SECTION 1 — THE GAP IS REAL (data-backed)

### Why this gap exists

Indian RC market has 3 clean tiers with NO bridge:

| Tier | Price | Brands | Customer |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| Toy-grade | ₹500–1,500 | Centy, Funskool, Sadar generics | Birthday gift, kid <8 |
| **🕳️ THE GAP** | **₹2,500–₹8,000** | **(this file)** | **Adult / teen who wants premium feel, can't justify hobby price** |
| Hobby-bridge | ₹10,000–25,000 | MJX Hyper Go, WLToys, Rlaarlo | Serious hobby entry |
| Hobby-grade | ₹25,000+ | ARRMA, Kyosho, Traxxas | Enthusiast |

| Sourcing channel | Wholesale cost | Retail price | Gross margin |
|------------------|---------------|--------------|--------------|
| Direct China (Yiwu/Shantou OEM) | ₹400–₹900 | ₹2,500–₹4,000 | **75-85%** |
| DeoDap / Sadar Bazaar | ₹600–₹1,200 | ₹2,500–₹4,000 | **60-75%** |

| Compare: Hemani Kyosho dealer | ₹19,000 | ₹29,193 retail | 35% gross |

**The math is brutal in BCH's favor.** A ₹2,800 sale at 70% margin earns the same gross as a ₹25,000 hobby sale at 8% margin. AND moves 5-10x faster.

### How to verify a SKU before bulk buying

1. **Order 1 sample** from DeoDap or IndiaMART supplier (₹600-1,500 + shipping)
2. **Drive it for 1 week** — check battery life, motor durability, steering precision, drift behavior
3. **Film the unboxing reel** — if it doesn't look ₹15K-premium on camera, drop the SKU
4. **Check parts availability** — ask the supplier if they sell spare wheels, motors, batteries
5. **Only THEN** commit to 10-20 unit bulk order

## SECTION 9 — RISKS + MITIGATION (gray-zone specific)

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Customer returns / "this isn't real hobby" complaints | Be honest in product description: "Premium look, hobby entry feel. Not for racing." Set expectation. |
| Brand erosion of BCH premium positioning | Separate Instagram handle (@bangalorergarage). Separate shelf with signage. Different photo style. |
| Race-to-bottom as others discover same suppliers | 6-12 month margin window. Use it to build content moat. By month 6, BCH should be the Bangalore RC content brand on Instagram. |
| Counterfeit IP risk (looks too much like Traxxas/ARRMA) | Only buy ORIGINAL design generics (Lambo/Ferrari/Tesla licensed via Rastar/Maisto, OR Chinese OEM designs that don't copy specific Traxxas SKUs) |
| Battery quality / fire risk | Insist on lithium battery with BIS mark or equivalent. If unsure, sell with disclaimer + warranty card. |
| GST / invoicing for cheap unbranded SKUs | Only buy from GST-registered suppliers (DeoDap, IndiaMART verified, Sai Trading). Never cash deals without invoice — BCH loses input credit and inventory traceability. |
| "Legend of Toys" doesn't exist as a brand | Use Rastar as the proxy — it's a verifiable licensed brand at the exact same price slot |
