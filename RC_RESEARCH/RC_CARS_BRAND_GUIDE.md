## 2. FUTABA ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — THE RADIO SYSTEM BRAND

**What it is:** Futaba is NOT an RC car brand. They make the **radio control systems** — the transmitter (handheld controller) and receiver (circuit board inside the vehicle). Think of Futaba as the "Shimano shifters" of RC — not the frame, but the control system that makes everything work.

Founded in Japan in 1948 (older than Kyosho). Futaba dominates RC radio systems globally alongside Spektrum. Every serious hobbyist who builds a kit car or upgrades their electronics reaches for Futaba.

**Country of origin:** Japan
**Founded:** 1948
**Category:** Radio Control Systems (NOT vehicles)
**Who uses Futaba:**
- RC airplane and helicopter pilots (biggest user base)
- Competitive RC car racers upgrading from OEM bundled transmitters
- RC boat racers
- Anyone who buys a Kyosho or Tamiya kit that comes without electronics

Known for: Rustler (the everyman basher), Slash (the stadium truck), Maxx (giant bash machine), TRX-4 (scale crawler), E-Revo (6-wheel monster).

## 5. DU-BRO ⭐⭐⭐ — THE ACCESSORIES BRAND

**What it is:** American accessories manufacturer (founded 1949, Wauconda, Illinois). Makes the small-part consumables that every RC hobbyist needs repeatedly: body klips, fuel tubing, pushrods, E/Z connectors, prop balancers, wheel collars, clevises, landing gear, thread-lock products. Think of Du-Bro as the "Park Tools" of RC — not the star of the show, but what every serious workshop stocks.

**Country of origin:** USA
**Founded:** 1949
**Category:** RC hardware and accessories — NOT vehicles

**India Distribution:**
- ⚠️ **No confirmed India distributor**
- Only verified Indian stockist: **Havoc Hobby Bangalore** (appears in their brand navigation)
- Not on Amazon India
- Not on IndiaMART
- For bulk inquiry: bulk@dubro.com (US-based contact)
- Import route: via Havoc Hobby sub-wholesale or direct from Du-Bro with air freight

**India Pricing:** Not verified in INR. Estimate ₹250–₹2,500 per item based on US retail ($2–$35) with India import margin.

**Why BCH should eventually carry Du-Bro:**
Body klips break. Fuel tubing degrades. E/Z connectors bend. These are the consumables every Traxxas/ARRMA/Kyosho owner needs repeatedly. One customer with 3 RC cars = Du-Bro customer 4x/year. High margin, high frequency, zero marketing required — the customer knows what they need and asks for it.

**BCH Strategy:**
- **Phase 1:** Skip — don't complicate launch inventory
- **Phase 2 (Month 4-6):** Start with 5 SKUs: body klips, fuel tubing, E/Z connectors, thread-lock, wheel collars. Source from Havoc Hobby sub-wholesale initially.
- **Phase 3:** Establish direct Du-Bro account via bulk@dubro.com

**BCH Verdict: Phase 2 spare parts wall. Low-ticket, high-margin, recurring revenue from existing RC owners.**
