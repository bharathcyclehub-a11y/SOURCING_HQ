## SECTION 0 — METHODOLOGY

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| `vidiq_ig_profile` | Profile metadata (followers, bio, verification) | 5 credits |
| `vidiq_ig_profile_reels` | Top 6 reels (popularity-sorted from recent activity, API-capped at 6) | 5 credits |
| `vidiq_ig_reel_watch` × 6 | Per-reel: transcript + visual breakdown + audio analysis + Five Forces scoring | 60 credits (10 ea.) |
| **Total** | 6 reels deeply analyzed | **70 credits** |

The Five Forces engagement framework scores each reel on:
- **Stop** — Does it halt the scroll in the first 1-2 seconds?
- **Gap** — Does it open a curiosity loop (unanswered question)?
- **Momentum** — Does pacing/density keep viewers watching?
- **Feeling** — Does it trigger a strong shareable emotion?
- **Reach** — Can a stranger understand it without prior context?

Each force rated **Strong / Moderate / Weak**.
