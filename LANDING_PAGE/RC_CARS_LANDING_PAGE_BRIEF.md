## 1. PURPOSE & KPI

- **Primary KPI:** prepaid orders. Secondary: COD orders. Tertiary: DM leads.
- **Conversion flow:** reel → comment "RC" → DM autoreply with landing-page link → land → buy (prepaid preferred, COD allowed).
- **Page does ONE job:** turn an Instagram-reel visitor into an order in under 90 seconds. Every section either sells or removes a doubt.

## 2. AUDIENCES (in priority order)

| # | Audience | Buying for | Hook that pulls them | What they need on page |
|---|----------|------------|----------------------|------------------------|
| 1 | **Gifting buyer** (girlfriend/wife/mother buying for a man) | Adult car-lover (18–40 male) | "Perfect gift for him" | Gift-box visuals, "delivered gift-ready", anchor MRP |
| 2 | **Parent** (mom/dad of 4–12 yr old) | Kids | Car-ride survival / screen-time swap | "Safe for kids", durability, easy controls, age guide |
| 3 | **Self-buyer / enthusiast** | Self | Drift / desk-aesthetic / specs | Scale + spec details, model variants, drift video |

**Hero copy must work for #1 first** — gifting is the proven highest-virality buyer (10M+ reels in competitor data).

## 4. CONVERSION MECHANICS (built into the page)

- **Comment-to-DM bridge:** the page exists *because* an IG reel CTA said "Comment RC for link". Auto-DM tool (Manychat / native IG) sends the landing-page URL when "RC" is commented. The page URL should be **short and brand-readable** (e.g. `bch.in/rc` or `bharathcyclehub.in/rc`).
- **Meta Pixel / GA4** on the page for retargeting + ad-creative attribution. *Critical:* this lets you boost the reels that converted, not just the reels that got views.
- **Cart abandonment WhatsApp ping** if Shopify/cart used.
- **Mobile-first** — 95%+ of Instagram traffic is mobile. Hero must work in a thumb-scroll within 0.5s.
