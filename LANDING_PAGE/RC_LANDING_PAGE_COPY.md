## §0 — BRAND VOICE QUICK REFERENCE

- **Tone:** confident, slightly playful, never saccharine. Hindi-English code-switch where it lands naturally; English-primary.
- **Person:** "you" to the buyer; "we" for BCH (never "I"); never "our team is committed to providing…" corporate-speak.
- **Rupees:** always with ₹ symbol, no spaces (`₹1,199`). Indian comma format (`12,000` not `12000`).
- **Numbers:** spell out under 10 in body copy ("six months"); numerals in trust strips and prices ("12,000+ orders").
- **MRP/discount:** always struck-through MRP + actual price + saved-amount in rupees first, % second.
- **Emojis:** sparingly — 🎁 for gifting CTAs, 🔥 for offers, ⭐ for hero badge. No 🚀 / 💯 / 🤝 — feels off-brand.
- **Don't:** "hurry up", "ONLY 17 LEFT" with caps, fake countdowns, "as low as", "best-in-class".
- **Do:** specific verbs ("drifts", "ships in 24 hrs", "auto-confirms COD"), specific numbers ("45-min runtime"), specific places ("from Bangalore").
