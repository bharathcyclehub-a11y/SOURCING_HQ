### 🎯 Reach takeaways for Pocket RC Cars

1. **YouTube partnership trumps own-channel.** Hobby Central + chatpat (10.1M), Tygatec + HACKER JP (3M), Bharat Hobby + Happy Here (779K) — ALL the top reach plays in India are creator-led, not brand-channel-led. **Pocket needs 1-2 partner creators by Day 30.**
2. **Daddy Drones' 981K IG = the ceiling.** Their pattern: founder-led face, daily content, unboxing-with-test format. **The site should embed an Instagram reel feed in the hero section.**
3. **Hindi-language content wins virality.** Peephole 845K + HGT's 9.8M-view "Dilado yr" reel both run Hindi. **Site copy should support Hindi-English code-switch microcopy.**

### 🎯 SEO takeaways for Pocket RC Cars

1. **Best-in-class = Legend of Toys.** Steal: Shopify schema markup, product page structure, alt text on every image, category pages built around buyer intent (gifting, parenting, hobbyist).
2. **Domain-hack worth knowing: Hemani's kyoshoindia.com.** Exact-match domains still work for narrow brand queries in India. **Reserve `pockethobby.com`, `pocketdrones.com`, `pocketrcindia.com` defensively now.**

4. **Blog is mandatory.** Legend of Toys + Tygatec rank because they have content depth. Panday must build blog architecture into Site 1 — even if empty at launch, the structure must exist.

### 🎯 Sales takeaways for Pocket RC Cars

1. **Brand+funding+factory wins.** Mirana + LoT raised ₹78.5 Cr combined because their model = brand + manufacturing + content. **Pocket must show all 3 in the founder story on the About page.**

3. **Marketplace volume ≠ brand.** Baybee, Toyzone, Webby do ₹15-20 Cr each on marketplaces with weak brand recall. **Don't chase volume at the cost of brand. Stay D2C-first.**

### 🎯 Conversion takeaways for Pocket RC Cars

1. **Best-in-class = Legend of Toys.** Steal: hero product with 200+ review counter, "Made in India" + "BIS" badges in row of 4 trust icons, magnetic-close packaging hero shot, sleeve animation on PDP, sticky Add-to-Cart on mobile.

4. **Review velocity is the conversion moat.** Legend of Toys' 238 reviews on Flare 2.0 = the single biggest conversion lever in this category. Pocket must seed 30+ reviews from a soft-launch list before public launch.
