## PART 3 — UNIT ECONOMICS & THE ₹999 ENTRY

### Per-unit math (your numbers)

| Line | ₹999 entry model | ₹1,199 hero model |
|------|------------------|-------------------|
| Sell price | 999 | 1,199 |
| Landing cost | −570 (avg of 520–620) | −570 |
| Shipping (prepaid avg) | −85 | −85 |
| Packaging | −25 | −25 |
| Payment gateway (2%) | −20 | −24 |
| **Contribution before ads/RTO** | **₹299** | **₹495** |

**Takeaway:** the ₹1,199 hero model carries the business (₹495/unit). The ₹999 is the *gateway hook* — it gets the click and the "starts at ₹999" headline, but you must upsell to ₹1,199 (drift + LED + extra wheels) on the product page / in DM.

### RTO is the silent killer
COD orders in India RTO (return-to-origin) at 25–40%. Each RTO ≈ ₹150–200 loss (two-way shipping). **Mitigation:**
- Push **prepaid** with a ₹100 discount ("₹1,099 prepaid / ₹1,199 COD").
- COD confirmation call/WhatsApp before dispatch.
- Target ≤20% COD share.

The wholesale account also becomes a full product catalog: one post per model, every model, each captioned as a wholesale offer.

### Why ~15, not 1, not 50
- Only **~10% of reels go viral** (your own observation — confirmed: ~5 of 49 reels here drove most of the views). You need *volume of attempts*. 15 accounts × 2 reels/day ≈ 30 shots/day ≈ ~900 reels/month → statistically ~90 will pop.
- 15 is near the ceiling for solo management. Beyond this, accounts get thin/spammy → Instagram throttles them. If bandwidth is tight, run the 2 parenting pages + 2 hubs at full cadence and let the generic creator accounts post less often.
- With 4–5 models in the SKU, each account features different models so the network doesn't look like copy-paste.

### Content flooding workflow
1. Shoot **5 base reels** per model (gifting, desk, drift, unbox, price-reveal).
2. Re-cut each with different audio/caption/hook → 20–30 variants.
3. Spray across all 12 accounts over the week.
4. Whichever reel/account pops → (a) pin it on brand hub, (b) boost ₹500–1000, (c) DM-funnel everyone who comments.
5. Kill dead reels fast. Double down on the winning hook.

### How many models to show
Your SKU has 4–5 models — that is *enough*. Don't add SKUs yet. Each model = a different licensed body (Challenger / GTR / Supra / BMW / Porsche). Rotate them so content stays fresh; let the audience pick the bestseller, then concentrate stock on it.
