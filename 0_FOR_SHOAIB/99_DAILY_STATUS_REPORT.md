## Rules for the report

1. **No essays.** Bullets only. If you write a paragraph, Syed won't read it.
2. **Numbers > words.** Every report has counts: calls, visits, catalogs, samples, rupees.
3. **Don't hide blockers.** If a vendor ghosted you, say so. If you couldn't reach 3 of 7 numbers, say so. Hiding bad news always makes it worse.
4. **Don't ask for opinions. Ask for decisions.** Bad: *"What do you think about Taiba?"* Good: *"Should I door-knock Taiba next Tuesday? Y/N."*
5. **WhatsApp screenshots > paragraphs.** If a vendor sent a price list, paste the screenshot. If they sent a catalog photo, attach it. Don't retype.
6. **One message, one time per day.** Bundle everything into the 7 pm report. Don't ping Syed 8 times across the day. Exception: emergency / vendor about to walk away / spend decision >₹5K.
7. **No "I think" or "maybe."** Say *"Confirmed"* or *"Awaiting catalog"* or *"Dead phone."* Be a sensor, not a speculator.
8. **Spend reporting is non-negotiable.** Every rupee on a sample or auto fare goes in the daily total. Receipts in a folder.
