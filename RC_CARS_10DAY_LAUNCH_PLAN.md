### §5.8 Safety checklist (laminate at bench + demo zone)

**Child / demo safety:**
- Adult supervision mandatory under-13s. No exceptions.
- Closed-toe shoes on carpet. No food/drinks within 2m.
- Max 2 cars driving simultaneously on 5m × 1.5m strip. **No 1/10 or larger indoors. NEVER 1/8 brushless indoors.**
- Speed-cap to "low" mode for kids under 8. Display cars stay in cabinet — never driven indoors.
- Cones + foam barriers must be in place before any car powers on.

**LiPo battery safety:**
- All LiPo charging only inside fireproof bag, on service bench, never on display.
- Never charge swollen / punctured / hot battery — quarantine + dispose at e-waste vendor.
- Max storage charge 3.85V/cell for unsold LiPos.
- "Charging in progress — do not unplug" sticker visible during every charge.
- Fire extinguisher within 3m of LiPo charging station — verify monthly.
- Never leave charging LiPos unattended.

**What NOT to demo indoors:**
- 1/8 monster trucks (outdoor only)
- Anything >30 km/h on 5m strip
- Nitro/gas — never indoors
- Customer-owned cars without staff inspection (liability)
