# 02 — Field Visits: Mumbai + Delhi

**Swet — these are walk-in visits. Phone-call first if the number exists, otherwise show up cold and ask the manager.** Bring:

- Business card / Bharath Cycle Hub letterhead
- A printout of the BCH RC Mini Drift Car brand brief (Syed will share)
- Cash for petty samples (₹3,000 cap per shop)
- Notebook + this checklist
- A phone with WhatsApp + camera (photograph rate cards, shelves, MOQ sheets — but always ask "photo le sakta hoon?" first)
- Soft copy of BCH GSTIN + PAN on phone (vendors will ask)

**Two trips:**

- **Trip 1 — MUMBAI (3 days):** focus = importer warehouses + Mumbai hobby retail. Highest ROI because Maharashtra owns 65% of India's RC car imports (Taiba Mumbai 42% + Itoyy Bhiwandi 23%). This is where containers actually land.
- **Trip 2 — DELHI (1 day):** focus = Sadar Bazar Teliwara cluster for price-floor intelligence only. Product is toy-grade per our Gemini visual scan — we are NOT sourcing here, only benchmarking competitor pricing.

**Rule of engagement:** Never say "Bharath Cycle Hub launching a competing RC brand." Position as **"small Bangalore wholesaler exploring RC category for a 1-store retail business."** Don't burn bridges before we even open them.

**Reporting cadence:** Every evening, send Syed (a) photos of catalogs/rate cards, (b) the completed checklist entries below, (c) one-line summary per shop, (d) WhatsApp numbers of every person you met.
