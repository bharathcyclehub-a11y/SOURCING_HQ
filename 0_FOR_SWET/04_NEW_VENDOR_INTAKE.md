# 04 — New Vendor Intake Form

**When you discover a vendor not in `01_PHONE_CALLS_THIS_WEEK.md` or `02_FIELD_VISITS_MUMBAI_DELHI.md` — copy this form (use Ctrl/Cmd+D to duplicate the section), fill it in, and add a row at the bottom in the master log.**

*Why this file exists:* In a 4-week sourcing sprint you will discover 30–50 vendors that aren't on the master lists — from Instagram comments, vendor referrals ("call my cousin in Surat"), Google Maps side searches, a chai-shop tip. If they live only in your head or WhatsApp scroll, they're lost. Write them down here. Syed reviews this file weekly and decides which to escalate.

**Rule:** A vendor is only "discovered" once their row appears in the master log at the bottom of this file. WhatsApp screenshots don't count.
